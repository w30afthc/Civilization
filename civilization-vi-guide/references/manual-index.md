# Official Manual Index

Use this index for the two local official Civilization VI manuals:

- Traditional Chinese PC manual: `references/CIV_VI_Extended_Manual_(Trad_Chinese)_PC(For_TW)_SM.pdf`
- English 25th anniversary manual: `references/CIV_VI_25TH_ONLINE_MANUAL_ENG.pdf`

Both are 2016 base-game manuals, so treat them as standard-rules references. For this project, official manuals have the highest priority for base-game concepts, UI, keyboard shortcuts, and official wording. Use the Traditional Chinese manual first for Chinese terminology, and use the English manual to cross-check ambiguous translation or page context.

Use Civilopedia and the community wiki for exact data tables, current database entries, and details not explained in the manuals.

## How To Use

Answer the user in Simplified Chinese. Do not quote long passages from the manuals; summarize the rule and give the practical play implication.

When the user's question is about standard-rules fundamentals:

1. Check this index.
2. Inspect the Traditional Chinese manual page if the term exists there.
3. Cross-check with the English manual when wording is unclear.
4. Use Civilopedia or the community wiki for exact numbers, hidden data, or edge cases.

## High-Use Cross References

- **Keyboard shortcuts / 跳过回合**:
  - English manual p. 30.
  - Traditional Chinese PDF p. 16.
  - `Skip Turn` hotkey is `SPACE`; `Fortify` is `F`; `Sleep` is `Z`; `Delete Unit` is `DELETE`.
- **Amenities / 满意度/宜居度**:
  - English manual p. 95.
  - Traditional Chinese uses `Amenities（滿意度）`; negative sources around Traditional Chinese PDF p. 70.
  - Too few amenities reduce growth and yields; severe unrest stops growth and can spawn rebel partisans.
- **War weariness / 厌战情绪**:
  - English manual p. 139.
  - Traditional Chinese PDF p. 70.
  - Long wars, especially later eras, create war weariness; losing units worsens it.
- **Pillaging and repair / 劫掠与修复**:
  - English manual p. 120.
  - Traditional Chinese PDF pp. 59, 61.
  - Builders repair pillaged roads/improvements; a pillaged improvement and resource cannot be used until repaired. Traditional Chinese manual says repair takes one turn and does not consume a Builder action.
- **Pantheons and religion / 万神殿与宗教**:
  - English manual p. 124.
  - Traditional Chinese PDF p. 63.
  - Faith leads to founding a pantheon, then toward a religion with a Great Prophet.
- **City capture / 占领城市**:
  - Traditional Chinese PDF p. 44 has a useful summary: when city HP reaches 0, enemy melee units can enter and capture the city.
- **Air bases, aircraft, carriers / 空军基地、飞机、航空母舰**:
  - Traditional Chinese PDF pp. 77-78.
  - Air units must be stationed at a city center, aerodrome/airport, airstrip, or carrier; aircraft heal only when not involved in combat and when returned to an air base/carrier.
- **Nukes and contamination / 核武器与污染**:
  - English manual p. 155.
  - Traditional Chinese PDF p. 79.
  - Contaminated tiles damage units ending turns there and cannot be worked until the timer expires or a Builder cleans them.
- **Victory conditions overview / 胜利条件**:
  - English manual p. 146.
  - Base game includes science, culture, religion, and domination victory paths.
- **Science victory projects / 科技胜利项目**:
  - English manual p. 148.
  - Spaceport projects progress through satellite, moon landing, and Mars modules in the base game.

## Standard-Rules Caveat

These manuals predate Rise and Fall and Gathering Storm. They do not cover governors, loyalty, era score, disasters, climate, power, diplomatic favor, grievances, World Congress, or Gathering Storm-era strategic resource changes.
